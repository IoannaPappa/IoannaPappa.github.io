Master’s Thesis
Lightning Detection in Thunderstorms Using Artificial Intelligence
Lightning detection is a critical research area due to its risks to human life, infrastructure, and the environment. This master’s thesis explores the use of artificial intelligence for detecting lightning in thunderstorms. Traditional physics-based detection methods are reviewed, alongside AI approaches involving neural networks, image processing and computer vision. The experimental part implements and evaluates deep learning models YOLOv5 and YOLOv8, trained on a dataset of lightning images. The study analyzes their performance and demonstrates the potential of AI for real-time lightning detection. PDF available upon request
