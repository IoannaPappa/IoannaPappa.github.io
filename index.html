Research

Master’s Thesis. Lightning Detection in Thunderstorms Using Artificial Intelligence

This research investigates the application of deep learning models for real-time lightning detection. YOLOv5 and YOLOv8 architectures were implemented and evaluated on a curated lightning image dataset. The study explores AI-driven alternatives to traditional physics-based detection systems.
